B5.5 Increase your word power: -ed / -ing adjectives
A large group of adjectives are verb participles, with an -ing or -ed ending.
Examples:
interesting, interested
Adjectives with an -ing ending describe something.
Adjectives with an -ed ending describe the effect that thing has on you.
Example:
The lesson was interesting. We were all interested in what we were doing.
