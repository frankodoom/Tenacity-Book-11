5 Who do you study with?
Is it better to study alone or with someone else? Studying can be a lonely occupation, but if you
try to study with a friend, you may spend more time chatting than studying.
There are occasions when it is helpful to study with someone else:
 •to discuss a text you have both read
 •to discuss a new topic you don’t understand very well
 •to test each other, e.g. reviewing a unit in your text book.
If you do study with someone else, set a time limit and plan to do something fun afterwards
