A2.16 Study skills: Setting goals
English is the most commonly used language in the world for people who want to communicate with
others who don’t speak their first language. So if someone from Algeria wants to speak to someone
from China, and neither speaks the language of the other, then they will use English. English is also the
most commonly used language in written communication such as letters, emails, books, newspapers,
academic journals. So there are many reasons why English is a useful language to learn.
