B12.2 Language focus: Speculating about the future
1
Read this dialogue.
Murad: Do you think it’s possible computers will take over the work of human beings in the
future?
Halchitu: Oh yes. I’m quite sure they will. Computers already do so much for us. That’ll
definitely continue.
Murad: Really? It may happen, but I think it’s unlikely. There’s a good chance they’ll do more
than they do now, but I’m certain that human beings will carry on working in the future.
