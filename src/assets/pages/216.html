B8.2 Language focus: Adverbs
Work with a partner.
1
What is the difference between an adjective and an adverb? Discuss this and then look at
the table.
Adjectives and adverbs
Compare these two sentences;
The captain of the team was so efficient that everyone admired him.
The captain led his team so efficiently that everyone admired him.
Adjectives can be used before a noun, They can also be used after certain verbs These are link verbs
(verbs which refer back to the subject) for example;
Subject link verb adjective
He became famous
She was rich
They ended up successful
Adjectives can also be used after verbs of feeling and appearance
Subject verb adjective
She looked beautiful
The ugali tasted delicious
The room smelt horrible
