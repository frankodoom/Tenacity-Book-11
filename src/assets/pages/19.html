Specifically the African Union has set for itself the ambition of building by the year 2030:
“A united and integrated Africa; an Africa imbued with the ideals of justice and peace; an
inter-dependent and robust Africa determined to map for itself an ambitious strategy; an Africa
underpinned by political, economic, social and cultural integration which would restore to
Pan-Africanism its full meaning; an Africa able to make the best of its human and material
resources, and keen to ensure the progress and prosperity of its citizens by taking advantage of the
opportunities offered by a globalised world; an Africa capable of promoting its values in a world
rich in its disparities.”
Adapted from: STRATEGIC PLAN OF THE AFRICAN UNION COMMISSION Volume 1: Vision and
Mission of the African Union May 2004. Prepared by the African Union Commission 2004.
