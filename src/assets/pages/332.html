There have been other unmanned space flights to other planets in the solar system. For example, there
have been several missions to Mars, our nearest neighbour. Pathfinder explored the surface in 1997 and
Odyssey in 2004 brought back samples of rock which showed that many years ago the planet had the right
conditions to support life.
China became the third country to put men in space with the Shenzhou programme. In 2003 Yang Liwei
orbited the Earth in Shenzhou 5. In 2005 Shenzhou 6 carried two men in space for five days. Then in 2008, a
Chinese astronaut walked in space during the Shenzhou 7 mission.
