B8.9 Writing: Saving water in Ethiopia
Write two to three paragraphs (not more than one side of a page) about the best water
conservation method or methods;
• EITHER for your region or town
• OR for Ethiopia as a whole.
• Draw on the information in the Reading text B 8.8 and work you have done in this unit on water
shortage.
• You can also draw on other sources and your own ideas, if you wish.
• Describe the method or methods you are recommending and explain why they will bring benefits.
• Make a plan of your main points and supporting points.
• Write a first draft and check it carefully before writing a final version.
