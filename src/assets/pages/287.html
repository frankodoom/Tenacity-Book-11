B11.4 Speaking: Pronunciation – Minimal pairs
1
Listen to your teacher and underline the word you hear in each pair.
a fill feel
b soot suit
c farm firm
d litter litre
e pull pool
f late led
g heart hurt
h pain pen
2
Match each of the pairs of words above to these pairs of vowel sounds.
Group 1: / ɪ / / i: /
Group 2: / eɪ / / e /
Group 3: / ɑ: / / ɜ: /
Group 4: / ʊ / / u: /
3
Repeat the words after your teacher.
4
Test your partner. Take it in turns to read one word from each pair in exercise 1.
The other person must guess which word it is.
5
With your partner make a sentence for each pair of words.
Each sentence must contain both of the words in the pair.
Example:
Don’t put that litre bottle in the litter bin!.
