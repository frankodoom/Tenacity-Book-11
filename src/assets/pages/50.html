B2.1 Speaking: Who is the surgeon?
Work in a small group. Read this text and try to find the solution to the problem.
A surgeon and his son were driving fast in the family’s new car when they were involved in an
accident. They were both seriously injured. In fact, on arrival at the hospital, the surgeon was
dead. The boy was taken into the operating room for emergency surgery. A surgeon came in to
perform the operation, looked at the boy’s face and said, “I cannot operate on this child. He is
my son.”
The question is, who was the surgeon who didn’t want to perform the operation?

minutes and then answer the questions below.
