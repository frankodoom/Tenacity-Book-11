A1.4 Speaking: Pronunciation – word stress
The nationality ETHIOPIAN has five syllables: E – THI – O – PI – AN.
The third syllable is stressed or stronger than the others: Ethi'Opian
Generally, the stress pattern of a nationality is the same as that of the country name. There are some
exceptions, though!
