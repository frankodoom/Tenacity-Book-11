We can also use some conditional structures to give advice
g If I were you, I’d …
If I were you I wouldn’t …
This is very commonly used. It sounds quite friendly as you
are putting yourself in the position of the person you are
advising.
If I were you, I’d be more careful.
h I would advise you to …
I wouldn’t advise you to …
i I would recommend you to …
I wouldn’t recommend you to …
This is very formal and polite.
I would advise you to change your lifestyle as you are putting
yourself at risk.
j My advice would be to … This is also quite formal.
My advice would be to see a doctor as soon as possible.
Asking for advice
We can ask for advice using most of the structures above:
Do you think I should …?
Do you think I can …?
I’ve got a problem. What should I do?
I’ve got a problem. What can I do?
What would you do?
What would you advise me to do?
Could you give me some advice about …?
