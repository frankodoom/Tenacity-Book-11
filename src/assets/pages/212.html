Reading skills
Different reading skills are used for different kinds of reading tasks and hence for different kinds of texts.
With your partner, study this list of reading tasks and decide which of the skills in the mind
map would be most appropriate for each one. You can choose more than one skill for any of
the tasks.
a Reading a menu in a restaurant to find out what is served.
b Finding information on a specific topic in an encyclopedia or website.
c Reading the information you want in an encyclopedia or website.
d Looking to see what’s in the newspaper.
e Reading a newspaper article.
f Reading a text and answering comprehension questions about it.
g Reading a novel.
