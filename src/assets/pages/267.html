B10.5 Language focus: Revision
1 Cause and effect
We have already looked at cause and effect, which can also be referred to as reason and result.
See A7.7
We can link these two ideas by means of a linking word or expression, or a verb.
Cause Effect
Money alone does not lead to development.
Effect Cause
Development is not due to money alone.
