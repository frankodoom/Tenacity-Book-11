Phase 1: Group discussion – What are the factors that contribute to development?
a On your own look back at the list you made in A10.1 on the factors that contributed to South
Korea’s development and the notes you made in B10.1 on development.
b Work in a group. Together, make a list of factors that contribute to development: include some
of your own ideas as well as those that have been mentioned in this unit.
c Present your list to the rest of the class.
d Each group in the class should now select one of the factors that has been mentioned so far.
e In your group, discuss
i) the contribution your factor makes to development
ii) how it could help Ethiopia’s development.
f Make a simple mind map of your ideas about the factor you have been discussing.
g Everyone in the group must have a copy of the mind map and be able to defend the points the
group has made.
