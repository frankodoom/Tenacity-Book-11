c We use going to to express something we have already decided to do.
Why are you wearing those old clothes?

Note: You must use shall instead of will with I and we in questions.
Shall I come to your house on my way to school?
Shall we go and see if Rosie is at home?
It is rare nowadays to use shall with I and we, in statements.
We shall be early for the party. (rare) / We will be early for the party. (usual form)
